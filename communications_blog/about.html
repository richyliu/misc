About Me

Richard Liu — Troop Blogger

I have been blogging about Troop 485 events since I became webmaster in February of 2016. I have covered a variety of topics such as summer camps, rafting, Rim of the Bay campouts, etc. I also maintain the troop 485 website along with Jeffrey Meng

I hope to pass on this tradition of bloggin to future webmasters and keep this sacred art alive. This blog is a means of tracking our troop's progress thourghout the years by highlighting both achievements and failures.

If you would like to blog for our troop, please contact me at bsa485@gmail.com. We are looking for passionate writers with experience in journalism and/or online multimedia.
